WHO WE ARE

We are agile, adaptable and dynamic in our approach to our real estate transactions. Our diverse holdings span across Quebec, Ontario, New York, and Vermont, strategically positioned for optimal opportunities.
Our diversified portfolio contains 50+ properties, exceeding a 10 million square feet. Our array of property assets include office spaces, versatile commercial/industrial properties, shopping centers, land for development, self-storage facilities and residential projects.
We take immense pride in our extensive expertise in the real estate development sector, especially in transforming distressed properties into success stories.

WHAT WE DO

Our core focus is cultivating long-lasting relationships. The strong relationships with our tenants are instrumental in our yearly growth. We have dedicated ourselves to acknowledging the unique needs of each tenant in the face of ever-changing business cycles.
Through our collaboration with real estate professionals, we have honed a distinct skill in discovering exceptional real estate opportunities. We are proud to rejuvenate and transform challenging real estate situations and build remarkable additions to the contemporary landscape.
